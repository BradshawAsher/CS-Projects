AK-12

Price: $1000

Description

The AK-12 is chambered in 5.45×39mm, features a barrel length of 415 mm (16.3 in), a maximum firing range of 800 m (870 yd), and a standard magazine capacity of 30 rounds.

This gun is available every day, with customizations available. The AK-12 represents the Russian military entrance into the realm of the modern carbine. Think of the AK-12 as a modernized variant of the AK 74 rifle. It’s very much a modern variant of the classic AK series, while adopting some of the modularity of the M4 to push the old design forward. With an AK-12, you'll have your upgraded Russian Communist version of America's no recoil M4!
